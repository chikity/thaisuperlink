Introduction We came to work in Thailand from Hong Kong many years ago. Over the years we have casually helped friends and friends of friends do errands such as collecting rent for their rental properties, arranging repairs and even renovation of apartments, making payments for products and services that they purchased, coordinating delivery of their purchases, renewing annual license for their cars, etc. Lastly, when they visit Thailand we bring them to local restaurants that they can enjoy tasty food at reasonable (non tourist) prices! We believe we are in a position to provide tremendous help to people who are starting to deal with Thailand for long term prospects, or simply need something done correctly and efficiently as a one time event, eg. wedding photos, etc.. Hence the idea of offering our service as a profession!
