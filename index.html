What is ThaiSuperLink

We came to live in Thailand many years ago. Over the years we have casually helped friends and friends of friends do errands such as collecting rent for their rental properties, arranging repairs and even renovation of apartments, making payments for products and services that they purchased, coordinating delivery of their purchases, renewing annual license for their cars, arranging transportation for sightseeing trips, etc. We even arranged and coordinated wedding photography sessions.

Most importantly, when they visit Thailand we recommend local restaurants that they can enjoy tasty food at reasonable (non tourist) prices! Most of these restaurants are much loved by local people but unknown to tourists.

We believe, with our language ability, local experience and helpful attitude, we are in a position to provide tremendous help to people who are starting to deal with Thailand for long term prospects, or simply need something done correctly and efficiently as a one time event,such as taking wedding photos, etc.. Hence the idea of offering our service to those who need help!
